About The Pizza Corner

The Pizza Corner began with founder Mary Bridges. Mary had a passion for pizza and decided to create something to bring others that also have that same love. Today, The Pizza Corner has a regular user count of 20,000 a month, with meetings commonly bringing in 200 fans each. Every month, users can vote on their favorite pizza place, and pizza recipes to feature on the site - creating a constantly evolving and active commmunity.

Stay tuned into The Pizza Corner on Facebook, Instagram, and Pinterest!
